Ingredients

  • Lasagne Pasta
  • Garlic
  • Zucchini
  • Onion
  • Mushrooms
  • Red Lentils
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Spices
  • Tomato Paste
  • Water

Steps

  1. Prepare the bologna sauce, start by frying the onion and then the garlic in oil/water
  2. Fry the zucchini and mushrooms a little, then add the lentils and spices, add a little water so that they don't burn
  3. when they have softened a little, pour the tomato juice over them and cook until everything has softened completely
  4. stir occasionally and add a little water if necessary
  5. for the bechamel, mix the oatmeal, spices and brewer's yeast flakes in the cold milk until there are no lumps, then boil carefully, stirring slowly, until it thickens
  6. layer the lasagna in a heat-resistant dish: a layer of pasta, a layer of bologna sauce, a layer of béchamel - we got 3 layers, the top was smeared with béchamel
  7. Coverd and bake for 40 minutes at 180 degrees, then roast uncovered (those who eat vegetable cheese can also sprinkle it on top)
